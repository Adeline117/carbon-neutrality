## Interpretation

- **BCT's Lemons Index (~0.75+)** confirms extreme adverse selection — the pool's average credit is below BB.
- **CHAR's Lemons Index (~0.22)** shows the biochar allowlist filter successfully prevents quality degradation.
- **The gap between BCT and CHAR** (~0.5 index points) quantifies the value of quality gating.
- **The hypothetical AAA pool** shows the floor (~0.10) — even pure CDR doesn't reach LI=0 because vintage variation and per-project adjustments reduce composites below 100.
