This is a substantial improvement over the REDD+-only correlation (+0.664) and slightly below the cross-type-only (+0.906). The expanded dataset spans B-to-AAA on our scale and D-to-AAA on BeZero's, providing much wider dynamic range. The strong positive correlation demonstrates that our framework tracks BeZero's quality ordering across the full spectrum of project types.

## 7. Data Collection Notes

### 7.1 BeZero (26 of 30 projects)
BeZero publishes all headline ratings publicly at bezerocarbon.com/ratings/listings (677+ projects as of Apr 2026). This is the most extensive public disclosure of any agency. Our dataset captures projects where the specific rating is documented in press releases, case studies, or their public listings page.

### 7.2 Calyx Global (10 of 30 projects)
Calyx opened its platform to public access in June 2025, but granular project-level ratings (AAA-D) remain subscriber-only. Our Calyx data comes from: (a) CMW 2023 Table 20 (NZM scale, 6 REDD+ projects), (b) public research reports naming specific projects with ratings (BURN/Key Carbon AA, Arborify A, C-Quest Capital E), and (c) the CCP label tracker.

### 7.3 Sylvera (7 of 30 projects)
Sylvera's project-level ratings are largely behind a paywall. Our data comes from: (a) CMW 2023 Table 20 (NZM tier scale, 6 REDD+ projects), and (b) the Exomad Green press release (AA). Sylvera launched biochar ratings in late 2024 and may have additional public data points for biochar projects.

### 7.4 MSCI (1 of 30 projects)
MSCI Carbon Project Ratings cover 4,400+ projects but are paywalled. The only public project-level rating found is Rebellion Heartland 3 (AA), disclosed via press release.

### 7.5 Selection bias
This dataset is biased toward high-profile and higher-quality projects. Low-quality projects (D/C ratings) are underrepresented because developers do not publicize poor ratings. The REDD+ subset has better representation of the low end (Kariba D, Cordillera Azul C, Brazil Nut Concessions C) from investigative journalism and watchdog reports.
