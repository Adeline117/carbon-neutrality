## Insurance industry connection

Oka, Howden, Lockton, and WTW have all launched carbon credit insurance products (invalidation, reversal, non-delivery). They consume quality ratings as underwriting inputs. **Our composite + P(grade) is exactly what actuarial models need.** The quality-to-insurance pipeline theorized by Cabiyo & Field (PNAS Nexus 2025) is now commercial reality.

## Asian market gap

No independent commercial carbon rating agency exists in Asia. Singapore's NEA appointed BeZero/Calyx/Sylvera (all Western). Vericap (Singapore, 2024) is early-stage. China's CCER has government-run VVBs, not commercial raters. **Potential first-mover opportunity for an on-chain quality standard in Asia-Pacific.**
