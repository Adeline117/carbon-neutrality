PRELIMINARY AUTHOR ATTESTATIONS — NOT ENDORSED
The ratings below are author judgment from public documentation, applied to 14 tokenized carbon credits on Base Sepolia testnet. They are not endorsed by Verra, Gold Standard, Puro, Isometric, ICVCM, BeZero, Sylvera, Calyx Global, or MSCI. Any value derived from these grades is play money. See deployment notes and the workshop paper for methodology.

1. Grade explorer

Pick a credit to see its v0.4.1 rating pulled live from (loading…) on Base Sepolia.

2. Grade probability distribution

No commercial rating agency publishes grade uncertainty. We do — P(grade) is computed from empirically calibrated per-dimension standard deviations via Gaussian variance propagation. A high P means the grade is robust; a low P (like Charm Industrial's ~57% AAA) means the grade is rater-dependent.
