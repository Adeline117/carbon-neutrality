# Community Validation Strategy

*How to validate the framework without traditional expert gatekeeping, following the CarbonPlan open-research model.*

## The CarbonPlan precedent

CarbonPlan (carbonplan.org) has established credibility in carbon market analysis without traditional peer review or expert panels. Their model:
1. **Publish analysis openly** with full methodology and data on GitHub
2. **Invite public challenge** via GitHub Issues and blog comments
3. **Respond transparently** to critiques with documented methodology diffs
4. **Build reputation through accuracy**, not institutional affiliation

Our framework can follow the same path.

## What this approach sacrifices

- **Regulatory pathway**: governments want institutional endorsement, not GitHub stars
- **Academic venue credibility**: top journals want peer review, not community validation
- **Speed**: community validation is slower than 5 expert emails

## What this approach gains

- **Transparency**: every critique is public and permanently documented
- **Reproducibility**: anyone can re-run the analysis and challenge specific numbers
- **Meritocracy**: the framework is judged on its outputs, not on who reviewed it
- **Scalability**: community validation scales; expert panels don't
