## Outreach Notes

1. **Lead with the questionnaire, not the paper.** The exec summary is 2 pages; the questionnaire is structured to be completable in 60-90 min. Send both, but emphasize the questionnaire.
2. **For commercial raters (C1-C3):** Frame as "academic peer review" not "competitive benchmarking." Emphasize that our framework is open-source and on-chain — a different market segment than their commercial off-chain products.
3. **For cited authors (A1-A5):** Lead with "your work is foundational to our framework; we want to make sure we've interpreted it correctly."
4. **For DeFi teams (D1-D4):** Lead with the GitHub repo and smart contracts. They will evaluate code before reading the paper.
5. **For buyers (E1-E3):** Lead with "would you use this?" — the integration question (S8.1) is the hook.
6. **Batch outreach into two waves:** Wave 1 (priority 1-10) immediately; Wave 2 (priority 11-20) after Wave 1 responses start arriving, so Wave 2 can see the spectrum of Wave 1 views if permitted.
7. **The Nguyen (FPT University, hungnp30@fe.edu.vn) and Zhou (Shanghai Jiao Tong) citations** are lower-priority for review — their papers are methodologically adjacent but not core to the weight-calibration questions. Include only if the first wave yields fewer than 10 responses.
