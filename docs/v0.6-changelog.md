# v0.6 Changelog

## Summary

v0.6 addresses the five most vulnerable claims identified by the Plan agent's comprehensive review of v0.5. Four of the eight planned workstreams are completed in this release; three require external input (API keys, expert responses, web access) and are staged with infrastructure ready.

## Completed workstreams

### W2: Rubric refinement
- `07_registry_methodology.json`: 4 tiers → 2 tiers (CCP-eligible 75-85 / Non-CCP 25-50). ICVCM's published 36 CCP-approved methodology list is the binary classifier. 4 prose modifiers → 2 Boolean modifiers. Scoring space ~1920 → ~40 outcomes.
- `05_vintage_year.json`: removed pre-Paris override (redundant with decay formula; caused IRR disagreement).
- `06_co_benefits.json`: tightened 0-9 harm band with explicit 2-line criterion (community harm OR biodiversity harm, per published evidence).
- `tools/remap_registry_v06.py`: auto-remaps 45 credits' registry scores based on CCP status.
- Impact: zero grade flips across both pilots; C004 Charm slightly improved (P(AAA) 0.521 → 0.572).

### W5: EAS adapter
- `contracts/CarbonCreditRatingEASAdapter.sol`: relay pattern from the v0.4 design doc. Trusted-attester allowlist, schema verification, revocation checking, attestation decoding.
- `contracts/IEASMinimal.sol`: minimal EAS read interface.
- References Hypercerts evaluator registry and GainForest Ecocerts as implementation precedents (discovered in v0.5 deep research).

### W6: Composability demos
- `contracts/examples/KlimaRetirementGate.sol`: kVCM retirement quality gate (meetsGrade() check before burn).
- `contracts/examples/CHARQualityOverlay.sol`: augments CHAR's binary allowlist with continuous quality rating + quality-based fee discount (AAA -5%, AA -3%, A -1%).

### W7: Biodiversity safeguards
- `biodiversityHarm` disqualifier added to Disqualifiers struct (caps at BBB). Per Zeng et al. (Nature Reviews Biodiversity 2026).
- No pilot credits currently flagged; expert consultation (W3) should identify candidates.
